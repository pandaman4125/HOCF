Mission Statement

HOCF was created to serve the disabled communities and inspire them to find their inner champion physically, mentally, emotionally, and financially and help them on their journey to be their best self. Our mission focuses on helping motivated members of the disabled communities receive proper medical equipment and support they need to start and achieve the optimum fitness level through coaching, education, movement, and nutritional guidance.
